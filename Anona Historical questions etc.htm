When did Anona West And Anona East start? Anyone know the history?

Mike, My first meeting there was in 1975. I have second hand knowledge from my first sponsor "Jim Conorton", who helped establish Anona West. He later became a past president of our club.

Anona East's first meeting was started in a Ice Cream Shop in downtown Villa Park in the mid to late 60's. It then transitioned to the old train station @ Ardmore & West Park Blvd. in the early 70's. In the 80's they first leased and eventually purchased their current location. There's plenty of history between the two clubs. I'll see if I can compile an accurate history. ( A lot is anecdotal ) Below is where I first attended meetings. The old train station. It's been improved a lot!

 

 

 

Anona West Anon Center (Original Inc. Name) was incorporated on 01-15-1973 but had been established the prior year.

 

 

In 1978 The Trustees of Anona West Co-Signed and purchased from Anona East the original Anona West. Bad blood arose between East and West as the initial purchase price was considered quite inflated from the 1973 purchase price. (Almost triple from what I was told.)
